Procedure

The game proceeds in three phases

Placing

The players alternating place their men once per turn. If one player is able to place three men in a straigt line, he formed a mill and may remove one of an opponent's man, which is not in a mill. When all men are placed, phase 1 is finished.

Moving

Players now move their pieces to adjacent points and try to form mills like in phase 1. This is done until one player only remains 3 pieces.

Flying

When a player only has left 3 pieces, his movements are not longer limited to adjacent points. He may now set his pieces to any point of the field. The game ends, when one player has no pieces left
